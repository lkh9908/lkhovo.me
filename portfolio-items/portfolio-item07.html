Group Research Project

Detailed Face Model Generation

I joined this project in February 2021.

Our goal is to generate a detailed 3d face model using one photo as input.

Prior to joining the group, I had very limited knowledge of the project; therefore, I learned a lot during the process.

By reading and annotating multiple related papers, I obtained precious experience of how computer science-related research works.

By working and learning from people around me, I also tasted the life of a professional researcher.

My main task is to set up State of the Art projects and compare their result.

Image Comparison

To Compare Images, I Studied and Set up a Comparing System that incorporates:

SSIM (Structural Similarity), PNSR (Peak signal-to-noise ratio), LPIPS (Perceptual Similarity Metric) and CSIM (Cosine Similarity).

To further test the quality of the reconstructed faces, I also utlized algorithms such as that provided by FaceNet and Face++.
